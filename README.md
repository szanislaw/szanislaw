<td width="50%">

### 🔍 [POFMA NLP Classifier](https://github.com/szanislaw/pofma-sentiment-analysis)
Multi-label BERT classifier identifying actors involved in Singapore's POFMA (Protection from Online Falsehoods) notices.

**Highlights:**
- `0.847` validation accuracy · `0.781` F1-score
- 512-token chunking for long PDF notices
- NER-enriched actor extraction from headlines
- End-to-end scraping & PDF parsing pipeline

`PyTorch` `BERT` `HuggingFace` `BeautifulSoup`

</td>
<td width="50%">

### 🎧 [Audio Deepfake Detector](https://github.com/szanislaw/htx-dfa-prosodicxlsr)
Combines **XLS-R** embeddings with **Parselmouth-based prosodic features** to detect AI-synthesised voices in Singaporean English.

**Highlights:**
- 30% EER improvement over baseline
- RawBoost-style augmentation pipeline
- Evaluated on ASVspoof2019 with EER, ROC-AUC
- Designed for real-world acoustic conditions

`PyTorch` `XLS-R` `Parselmouth` `ASVspoof2019`
